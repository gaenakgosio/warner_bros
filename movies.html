Titanic

"Titanic," directed by James Cameron, is an epic romance and disaster film released in 1997. Set against the backdrop of the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic in 1912, the movie follows the love story between Jack Dawson, a charming artist from a lower-class background, and Rose DeWitt Bukater, a young socialite engaged to a wealthy businessman.
